The High Cost of a Rear-View Mirror

Lagging Indicators

Traditional reporting tells you why people left yesterday. It doesn't tell you who is at risk of leaving tomorrow.

Costly Surprises

Unexpected departures of key talent lead to knowledge drain, project delays, and a significant impact on team morale.

Ineffective Interventions

Without knowing the "why" behind an employee's flight risk, retention efforts are often generic and miss the mark.

The goal is to move from asking "Why did they leave?" to "Who is at risk, why, and how can we help?"

The Solution: Attrition Insights on Workday

A predictive analytics hub in Workday that forecasts turnover risk and diagnoses the specific drivers, enabling proactive talent retention.

Predict Flight Risk

Our ML model analyzes historical data to assign a clear risk score (High, Medium, Low) to every employee.

Diagnose the "Why"

Go beyond the score. Our transparent model shows the specific factors driving an individual's risk.

Enable Action

Empower managers and HR with the insights needed for targeted, meaningful, and effective retention conversations.
