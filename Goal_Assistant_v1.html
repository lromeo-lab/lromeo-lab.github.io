The Hidden Costs of Goal Setting

Administrative Burden

Managers and employees spend thousands of hours drafting, reviewing, and correcting goals, diverting time from strategic work.

Strategic Misalignment

Team and individual goals often disconnect from top-level objectives, leading to siloed efforts and wasted energy.

Inconsistent Quality

Without a standard, the clarity and impact of goals vary widely, making it difficult to measure true performance.

The traditional goal-setting process is a barrier to agility and high performance.
